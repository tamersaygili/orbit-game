Privacy Policy — Orbit Game

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Orbit Game ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application Orbit Game (the "App").

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Username (nickname): A display name you choose when registering on the leaderboard.
  • Game score: Your highest score, stored to display on the global leaderboard.
  • Device identifier: A randomly generated anonymous ID used to identify your account.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Display your score and username on the in-game leaderboard.
  • Allow you to compare scores with other players.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Firebase (Firestore)

We use Firebase Firestore to store leaderboard data (username and score). Firebase is a service provided by Google LLC. For more information, please review Firebase's Privacy Policy at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described in this policy (AdMob and Firebase services above).

Your username and score are visible to other players on the public leaderboard.

Data Retention

Your leaderboard data (username and score) is retained as long as you use the App. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
